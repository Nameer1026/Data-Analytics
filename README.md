# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

**Tools:** Power BI · DAX

An interactive dashboard analyzing 1,400+ employee records to identify the strongest drivers of attrition — giving HR a data-backed starting point for retention planning instead of relying on anecdotes.

## Business Problem
HR suspected attrition was rising in specific teams but had no structured way to test *why* — was it overtime? job satisfaction? demographics? This dashboard isolates the actual drivers.

## What It Does
- Tracks **attrition rate** broken down by department, demographics, overtime status, and job satisfaction
- Surfaces which factors correlate most strongly with employees leaving
- Interactive views let HR test hypotheses (e.g., "is overtime really the driver, or is it tenure?")

# Employee Attendance Analytics Dashboard

**Tools:** Power BI · DAX · Power Query

An interactive Power BI dashboard that turns raw attendance logs for 500+ employees into a real-time view managers can act on — replacing monthly manual reporting with live drill-down analysis.

## Business Problem
HR and department managers had no fast way to spot rising absenteeism or attendance issues until a monthly report was compiled — by which point the trend had already cost productivity. This dashboard gives managers same-day visibility instead.

## What It Does
- Tracks **attendance rate, absenteeism trends, and workforce utilization** across the organization
- Department-level **drill-down** so managers can isolate issues to a specific team
- Built entirely on custom **DAX measures** for rate calculations and trend comparisons

# Store Sales Analytics Dashboard

**Tools:** Power BI · DAX

An interactive sales analytics dashboard built on 10,000+ transactions, designed to cut the time it takes to spot underperforming regions and products from days to minutes.

## Business Problem
Sales leadership needed a faster way to see where revenue and margin were slipping across regions — without waiting on a manually assembled spreadsheet report each cycle.

## What It Does
- Tracks **revenue, profit margin, and regional performance trends** in one view
- **Interactive filters** let users slice by region, product, and time period instantly
- Custom **DAX measures** for margin %, period-over-period growth, and regional ranking
